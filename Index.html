A little about how I work.

I got into marketing because I was genuinely curious about what makes people pay attention — and what makes them pay money. That curiosity hasn't left. I spend a lot of time thinking about why campaigns succeed or fail, and I bring that thinking to every client engagement.

I work with a small number of clients at a time so I can go deep. You'll work with me directly — not get handed off to a junior after the sales call. I'm honest when something isn't working, proactive when I see an opportunity, and I write reports that are worth reading rather than ones designed to look impressive.
